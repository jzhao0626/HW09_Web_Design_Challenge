Humidity

The plot above represents the relation between humidity and latitude. There is not a clear trend found as the latitude varies. Most of the cities tend to have a humidity above 60%, while the humidity of cities with latitude between 0 to 40 and -40 to -20 tend to vary more. Cities with latitude between 40 to 80 often have a humidity between 80% to 100%, but there are some cities having a humidity of 0%.
